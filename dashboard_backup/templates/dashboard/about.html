About IT Department

The Information Technology Department at Engineering College is committed to excellence in education, research, and innovation. Established in 2001, our department has been at the forefront of technological education, producing skilled professionals who contribute significantly to the IT industry.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in Information Technology education and research, nurturing innovative professionals who can meet global challenges.

Our Mission

To provide quality education through state-of-the-art infrastructure, industry-relevant curriculum, and research opportunities that prepare students for successful careers in IT and related fields.
